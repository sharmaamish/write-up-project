Built-Up Heat Map – Future Chain

A Built-Up Heat Map is a visual representation of futures market data based on the relationship between Price movement and Open Interest (OI). It helps traders quickly identify bullish strength, bearish pressure, trend continuation, and reversals.

Explanation of Columns

Column Explanation Trading Insight
Contract Name of the futures contract Identifies the trading instrument
LTP Last Traded Price Shows current market price
Price Change Difference from previous close Indicates bullish or bearish move
% Price Change Percentage movement in price Measures strength of price move
OI Change Change in Open Interest Shows position buildup or exit
OI % Percentage change in Open Interest Strength of participation
Volume Total contracts traded Confirms price & OI action
Built-Up Derived from Price vs OI behavior Identifies long/short activity
Signal Final market interpretation Helps trading decision
Key Logic:
• Price ↑ + OI ↑ → Long Buildup
• Price ↓ + OI ↑ → Short Buildup
• Price ↑ + OI ↓ → Short Covering
• Price ↓ + OI ↓ → Long Unwinding
